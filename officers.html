About the Officers


The Vietnamese Student Association (VSA) at George Mason University was established in 1976 with the objective of serving three entities:


1) Its members within George Mason University
2) The Vietnamese community
3) The Asian-American Community

As officers of GMU VSA, we are here to help you as a GMU VSA members. We are all students from very different backgrounds business, to health, to the most technical majors, but our goal is to maintain and treat our members with the utmost respect and quality as your officers. And this here is a way for you guys to know who they are and maybe understand the words that these people live by. If you have any questions please refer to our email by pressing the "Get in Touch button below"
